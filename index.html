Development Manager Job Description

A development manager is an essential role in many large businesses. They serve as the point of contact for the customer, upper management and customers. They work as project managers scheduling and delegating tasks required to successfully complete the company's initiatives. A development manager will work with other department heads to establish project milestones and work to complete projects effectively and within the budget.
